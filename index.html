THIRSTY WORKS IN FOURS SIMPLE STEPS

1- SEARCH:

It's simple. Using Thirsty homepage, enter the area you want to contribute in tackling thirst, and click on search. Alternatively, click on the search link on the top menure to view the list of all areas.

2- LOCATE:

Get to know where the place is by clicking on the place profile. It's even more exciting. You can get the directions from anywhere in the country.

3- DONATE:

You can contribute in tackling thirst by just one click! Choose the method you want to donate with. You can choose your preferred donation way among 4 available ways.

4- RELAX:

Once the donation step is completed, you can now rest and enjoy your contribution in the community. If you like, you can receive notifcations regarding the impact of your contribution.
